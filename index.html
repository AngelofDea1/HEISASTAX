SHITPOSTING

In crypto, everyone's selling something.

Shitposting is how you sell without selling.

It's how you stay human in a space where everyone's overselling and underdelivering.

CT doesn't trust ads.

They trust whoever makes them laugh when everything's dumping.

Seven years in, I've learned shitposting builds more trust than a thousand "we're building" tweets ever could.

02. THE ART OF
BREAKING IT DOWN

Most web3 content is written for people who already get it.

I write for the person who doesn't, yet.

I use real-life analogies, relatable comparisons, zero assumed knowledge.

 The kind of explanation where someone finally goes "oh, that actually makes sense." and then they share it because they finally understand enough to explain it to someone else.

SPACE HOSTING

Nobody wants to listen to a dev read a whitepaper for an hour.
